# 👋 Hi, I'm Eric A. Booth

### **Research Director | Statistical Programmer | MEL Specialist**

I am a Senior Director of Research with over 20 years of experience in **Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)**. I specialize in designing complex studies, building automated statistical workflows, and leveraging data to inform state and national policy.

Currently, I serve as **President and Senior Director of Research at Far Harbor** and have recently joined the team at **Texas 2036** to drive data-driven policy insights.

### 🛠️ Core Expertise

- **Statistical Programming:** Expert-level Stata development and Python automation.
- **Study Design:** Complex sampling, multilevel modeling, and quasi-experimental designs.
- **Policy Research:** Deep domain expertise in Education, Behavioral Health, and Juvenile Justice.
- **Automation:** Engineering reproducible "end-to-end" data pipelines for policy stakeholders.
